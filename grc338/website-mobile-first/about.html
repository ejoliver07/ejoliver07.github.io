Our History

We are the Oliver family, owners of Fresh of the Press. With collective backgrounds in print, architecture, finance, and coffee, we chose to combine our strengths into a real-life passion project.

In October of 2020, we launched Fresh off the Press on Chorro Street in Downtown San Luis Obispo. We have grown from a family-run business to a staff of 25, and we thank you all for making our dream a reality. Thank you so much to all of you who have made this possible.

Our goal has always been to create a creative and safe space for people to exist in outside of their everyday lives. Design a mug, create a mug and drink some coffee with us.

We hope you love it here!
